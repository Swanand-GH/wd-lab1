Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

About

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India and a famous aerospace scientist. He is known as the "Missile Man of India" for his contribution to India's missile development programs.

Achievements

He played a major role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998. Dr. Kalam also received India's highest civilian award, the Bharat Ratna. He inspired millions of students through his books and speeches.
